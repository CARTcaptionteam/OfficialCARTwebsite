Our Services

Cart

CART (Communication Access Real-time Translation) service is the realtime translation of spoken word into text using a stenotype machine. Text produced by our CART professionals can be displayed on individuals’ laptops or computer monitors, projected on screens, or shown on other digital display systems. CART is used by hard of hearing or deaf people, most often to understand what is being said word-for-word in a setting such as a meeting or classroom. Our CART professionals will troubleshoot with you to find the best solution for your need, allowing you to see what is said.

Interpreting

For people who cannot speak English, are hard of hearing, or are deaf, interpreting services is essential to participation in court and judicial environments. At Access Resource, we are certified to provide CART and interpreting services in Minnesota courts, and you can count on our extensive courtroom experience to bring you linguistic access throughout the entire courtroom process.

Captioning

Broadcast captioning services convert the audio content of a television broadcast, news service, sporting event, or any other type of programming into readable text displays on a screen or monitor. Our high-quality and certified captioners will provide the utmost care and attention to your specific need. From religious celebrations to the next big game, we are here to serve you with professional and reliable captioning.

Transcription

Our transcription services provide customers with documents from interviews, meetings, or speeches. The audio sources we transcribe can be live video, in-person events, or other forms of media and transcription is most often used for business, legal, or medical services. We can provide you access in a wide variety of situations. Do you have an upcoming professional event that requires you to comply with requirements for access to video and media? Our transcription services are exactly what you need!
