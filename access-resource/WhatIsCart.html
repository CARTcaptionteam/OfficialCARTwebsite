What Is Cart?

CART captioning is technology used to convert speech to text, word for word, in real time. This service is beneficial to anybody who is deaf, hearing impaired, or anybody who would otherwise benefit from such a service. CART Captioning is recognized by the Americans with Disabilities Act as an assistive technology for communication access. CART captioning can be used in almost any setting where there is speech that can be converted to text. This includes educational settings (from elementary to graduate school), business, medical, religious settings, sports environments and many more. It provides access to communication in all environments through various means from use of a laptop, large screen projection, iPad or smart phone viewing.
