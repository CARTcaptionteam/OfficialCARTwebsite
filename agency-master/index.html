What can Access Resource do for you?

High Quality CART and Captioning Services

The mission of Access Recourse is to provide access through the use of high-quality captioning to individuals with hearing loss and others that benefit from this service.

Communication Access Realtime Translation (CART), also known as realtime captioning, is a word-for-word speech-to-text interpreting service for people with a hearing loss or who would benefit from this accommodation. Different from notetaking or systems that summarize information, CART provides a complete translation of spoken words and environmental sounds allowing the consumers to decide the information that is important to them.

What We Do

CART Access to communication in a variety of settings through the use of a laptop, large screen projection, iPad or smartphone viewing.
Captioning High-quality Broadcast Captioning services for news, religious, sporting events and all types of programming needs.
Interpreting Court interpreter services from a highly experienced and certified Minnesota Court Interpreter for CART.
Transcription Access to videos and other media by providing transcription services of audio or video information in a variety of situations.
